            Western Digital
            Technology Company
            Western Digital Corporation is an Very Large Cap Technology company headquartered in the United States. Western Digital Corporation has made sizeable number of venture capital investments.
            Western Digital's primary area of focus is Solid State Drive, Hard Disk Drive, NVM Express, Flash Memory, Risc V & Object Storage.
            Western Digital's primarily competes with Seagate, Toshiba, SK Hynix, Silicon Motion & Kioxia
